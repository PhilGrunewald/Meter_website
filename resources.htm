Working document

Activity decision tree

The challenge for the time-use app we are developing is to allow users to enter 'what they are doing' on a small smart phone screen. We try to avoid scrolling and use of keyboards as much as possible. If we can find five appropriate terms that each lead to five sub-terms, then three clicks would be enough to distinguish between over one hundred activities. This Excel document is a first attempt. Comments and suggestions for improvement are highly encouraged. Please email them to philipp.grunewald@ouce.ox.ac.uk. Thank you.
